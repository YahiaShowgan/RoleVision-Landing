# Privacy Policy for RoleVision

**Effective Date**: November 2025
**Last Updated**: November 2025

## Introduction

RoleVision ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the RoleVision Chrome extension (the "Extension").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Resume Data**
- You voluntarily upload your resume to the Extension
- This resume is stored **locally in your browser only**
- We do not have access to your resume or store it on our servers

**Job Application Tracking**
- When you mark jobs as "applied," this data is stored locally in your browser
- This information is not transmitted to our servers

**Settings and Preferences**
- Your filtering preferences (blocked companies, keywords)
- Display settings and UI preferences
- All settings are stored locally in your browser

### Automatically Collected Information

**Job Analysis Data**
- When you analyze a job, the job description and your resume are sent to OpenAI's API for processing
- This data is processed securely and is not stored permanently by us or OpenAI
- Job titles may be processed for quick scoring

**License Information**
- If you purchase a PRO license, your license key is validated through our secure validation service
- We store only the license key and validation status
- We do not collect payment information (handled by our payment processor)

**Extension Usage**
- We do not track your browsing activity
- We do not track which jobs you view or apply to
- We do not collect analytics or telemetry data

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

1. **Provide Core Functionality**
   - Analyze job descriptions against your resume
   - Display relevance scores and match analysis
   - Track your applied jobs (locally only)

2. **Validate Licenses**
   - Verify PRO plan subscriptions
   - Enforce usage quotas based on your plan

3. **Improve the Service**
   - Analyze aggregate, anonymized data about API usage patterns
   - Debug and fix technical issues

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- Your resume, settings, and applied jobs list are stored **only in your browser**
- This data is stored using Chrome's secure storage API
- This data never leaves your computer unless you explicitly trigger a job analysis

### Third-Party Services

**OpenAI API**
- Job descriptions and resume text are sent to OpenAI's API for AI-powered analysis
- This data is transmitted over secure HTTPS connections
- OpenAI processes this data according to their [Data Usage Policy](https://openai.com/policies/usage-policies)
- OpenAI does not use data from API customers to train their models

**License Validation Service**
- License keys are validated through a secure Cloudflare Worker
- We store only: license key, plan type, activation date
- No personal information is collected during validation

## Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Stored indefinitely in your browser until you clear it or uninstall the extension
- **Analysis Requests**: Not stored by us; processed in real-time
- **License Information**: Retained for the duration of your subscription plus 1 year for accounting purposes

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

- **Access Your Data**: All your data is stored locally and accessible to you through the extension
- **Delete Your Data**: Clear your data at any time through the extension settings or by uninstalling the extension
- **Opt-Out**: Stop using the extension at any time by disabling or uninstalling it

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

- **OpenAI**: Job descriptions and resume text for analysis purposes only
- **Legal Compliance**: If required by law, court order, or government regulation
- **Service Providers**: Trusted partners who help us operate the service (payment processors, hosting providers)

## Children's Privacy

RoleVision is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## International Data Transfers

If you use RoleVision from outside the United States, please be aware that your data may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Displaying a notification in the extension
- Posting the new policy on our website

## California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to request deletion of your personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell your information)
