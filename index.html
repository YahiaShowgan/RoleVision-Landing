‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is my resume data safe?
Yes. Your resume is stored locally in your browser only. It's never uploaded to our servers. When you request an AI analysis, your resume is sent encrypted (HTTPS) directly to OpenAI's API for processing, then the result comes back. We never see, store, or share your resume.
Does this violate LinkedIn's Terms of Service?
RoleVision is designed for personal, non-commercial use only. It enhances your browsing experience by adding AI insights‚Äîsimilar to ad blockers or password managers. We don't scrape data, automate applications, or redistribute LinkedIn content. However, users are responsible for complying with LinkedIn's ToS.
How accurate is the AI matching?
RoleVision uses OpenAI's GPT-4 models for analysis. While highly accurate, AI can make mistakes. Use scores and analyses as guidance, not absolute truth. Always review jobs yourself before applying.
Why is there a free tier limit?
Each AI analysis costs money (OpenAI API fees). The free tier covers casual job searching while keeping the service sustainable. Active job seekers can upgrade for unlimited access.
Can I use this on mobile?
Currently, RoleVision is a Chrome desktop extension only. Mobile browser extensions have limited capabilities. We're exploring mobile options for the future.
Do you track my job search activity?
No. We don't use analytics, tracking pixels, or collect data about which jobs you view. Your job search is completely private. The only data we see is license validation (if you purchase a paid plan).
What happens to my data if I uninstall?
All your data (resume, cached scores, application history) is stored locally in your browser. When you uninstall the extension, all this data is automatically removed from your device. We don't keep any copies.
How do I get support?
Email us at RoleVisionExt@gmail.com. Free users get community support (we'll respond within 48-72 hours). Premium/Pro users get priority email support.
